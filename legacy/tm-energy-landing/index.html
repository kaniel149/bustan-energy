FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does solar installation cost on Koh Phangan?
Our EPC price is ฿32,500/kWp fully installed. A typical hotel system (50-100kWp) costs ฿1.6M-3.2M. With our PPA option, the upfront cost is ฿0 — you simply buy electricity at ฿4.50/kWh, a 25% discount from PEA rates.
What is a PPA (Power Purchase Agreement)?
With a PPA, we install and own the solar system on your roof at zero cost to you. You buy the electricity the system generates at a fixed rate (฿4.50/kWh) — cheaper than PEA. After the contract period (15-25 years), the system becomes yours. It's risk-free solar.
How long does installation take?
Residential: 1-2 days. Commercial (20-100kWp): 3-5 days. Large projects (100kWp+): 1-3 weeks. The site survey and permitting process takes 2-4 weeks before installation begins.
What happens during cloudy days or monsoon season?
Koh Phangan receives an average of 4.5 peak sun hours daily year-round. During monsoon season (Oct-Dec), production drops ~20-30%, but you still generate significant power. Our systems include grid-tie, so you seamlessly draw from PEA when needed.
Do I need building permits for solar panels?
For systems under 200kWp on existing buildings, the permitting process is straightforward. We handle all paperwork including PEA application, electrical inspection, and net metering setup.
What warranty do you offer?
LONGi panels: 25-year performance warranty + 12-year product warranty. Huawei inverters: 10-year warranty. Our installation: 5-year workmanship warranty. We also offer optional extended maintenance packages.
Can solar panels withstand tropical storms?
Yes. Our mounting systems are engineered for wind speeds up to 180 km/h. LONGi panels are tested for severe hail and extreme conditions. We use marine-grade aluminum mounting rated for coastal environments.
