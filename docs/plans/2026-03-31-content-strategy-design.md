## Content Types

### Reels (2/week)
- Kinetic typography with stats ("$47,000 saved in 10 years")
- Satellite scan animation — "We scan your roof from space"
- Bill reveal format — "$400 → $12/month"
- Myth busting with on-screen text
- AI renders of before/after installations
- **Hook rule:** First 3 seconds = text overlay + motion, always

### Carousels (1/week)
- ROI calculator visual (system cost → savings → payback)
- "5 questions to ask before going solar in Thailand"
- "PPA vs EPC — which model is right for your property?"
- Grid comparison: solar vs. generator vs. grid
- **Design:** branded slides, dark bg, gold accents, data-heavy

### Static/Lifestyle (1/week)
- AI renders: luxury villa + solar + tropical setting
- Dashboard screenshots showing real-time production
- Brand quotes + energy facts
- "Did you know?" format with Bustan Energy branding

## Content Ratio

- **80% value** — education, frameworks, behind-the-scenes, data
- **20% promotional** — case studies, CTAs, consultations (only after day 60)

## Research Insights

### Competitive Landscape
- Canvas Solar, Entropy Solar, Kunini — all under 500 IG followers
- No premium solar brand exists in the Thai island market
- Bustan Energy can be #1 with consistency alone

### What Works in Solar Content
- Money hooks > environment hooks ("$0 bill" beats "save the planet")
- Negative framing outperforms positive ("You're doing this wrong" > "Try this")
- Saves and shares > likes in algorithm ranking
- 7-30 second Reels with jump cuts get 32% more engagement
- Trending audio = 42% higher engagement
- On-screen text mandatory (50% watch without sound)

### Premium B2B Content Principles
- "Visibility gets you considered. Trust gets you chosen."
- Teach publicly, invite privately
- Every comment thread is a potential sales conversation
- Founder-led content gets more reach than brand accounts (add later when real content exists)
