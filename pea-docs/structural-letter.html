4. Engineer's Declaration (คำรับรองของวิศวกร)

I, the undersigned licensed Civil Engineer registered with the Council of Engineers of Thailand, have personally conducted a structural adequacy assessment of the existing roof structure of the three (3) factory buildings described above, and hereby certify the following:

1. The existing roof structural system (steel primary frame, purlins and cladding) has been inspected and evaluated for the additional dead load imposed by the proposed 84.5 kWp solar PV array (approximately 11.5 kg/m² including mounting hardware), together with wind uplift in accordance with DPT 1311-50 (Department of Public Works and Town & Country Planning — Thailand Wind Load Code) for the Koh Phangan zone.

2. The structure has been found to be structurally adequate to safely support the additional permanent load and imposed loads (wind, maintenance) without requiring reinforcement, provided that:

  • Mounting hardware is anchored into roof purlins (not into cladding alone) using stainless-steel self-drilling screws of min. Ø6.3 mm × 50 mm, min. 2 fixing points per L-foot;
  • Panels are installed with minimum 500 mm setback from all roof edges, ridges and eaves;
  • Equipotential bonding and lightning protection are implemented per TIS standards;
  • Minimum 600 mm maintenance walkway is preserved between panel rows.

3. This certification is valid for the installation described and is based on a visual + structural review of the existing construction, site measurements and available construction drawings.

4. I accept professional liability for this certification in accordance with the Engineer Act B.E. 2542 (พ.ร.บ. วิศวกร พ.ศ. 2542).
